About Safety Pilot

Safety Pilot is a next-generation traffic education system designed to make learning road safety intuitive, engaging, and effective. It combines cutting-edge technology with educational principles to improve awareness and skill levels of drivers at all stages.

Our Vision

To revolutionize road safety through immersive and intelligent training systems that reduce traffic accidents and save lives.

Our Technology

Safety Pilot is powered by Unreal Engine for high-quality simulations and Azure AI for adaptive learning experiences. It features real-time performance tracking, multi-language support, and an upcoming multiplayer mode.

Who It’s For

  • New Drivers – Build confidence and skill
  • Experienced Drivers – Refresh and update knowledge
  • Driving Instructors – Monitor and guide student progress
  • Traffic Authorities – Assess readiness and promote awareness
