Welcome to Safety Pilot

Safety Pilot is a gamified, AI-driven traffic education platform that helps users learn, practice, and master traffic rules through realistic simulations and personalized feedback. Whether you're a new driver or an experienced one, Safety Pilot makes road safety engaging and effective.

Key Features

  • Immersive 3D Traffic Simulations
  • Real-Time AI Feedback
  • Interactive Learning Modules
